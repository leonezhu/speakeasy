🌍 Cultural Notes

'I'll have...' is the standard way to order in English, not 'I want...'
Dietary restrictions are common and expected: vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, nut allergy.
'Check, please' is the universal way to ask for the bill at restaurants.
In many English-speaking countries, you don't call the waiter by snapping fingers — make eye contact and raise your hand slightly.
